About Me

I hold a degree in telecommunications engineering, specializing in radiocommunication. My career has allowed me to develop a variety of skills and acquire solid experience in the field. During my two (02) month internship at the electricity company ADES, I participated in the maintenance and deployment of electrical networks, allowing me to understand the interconnection between electrical systems and telecommunications infrastructures.

Subsequently, I completed another nine (09) week internship at CAMTEL, where I deepened my technical knowledge by working on projects related to the optimization and maintenance of telecommunications networks. This experience allowed me to better understand the challenges associated with managing large-scale networks and implementing effective technical solutions.

In addition, I completed the Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) training, during which I validated the CCNA 1 and 2 certifications. This training allowed me to master the basic and advanced concepts in networking, which strengthens my ability to intervene effectively on telecommunications infrastructures. Currently, I am retraining in my second passion which is web development.
